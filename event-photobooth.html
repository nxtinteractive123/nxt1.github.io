Event Technology - Photobooth Solutions

Augmented Reality - Photobooth

Augmented Reality is one of the most exciting tools in the marketing toolkit. Interactive by nature, it deeply engages audiences and invites them to connect with brands in a flat-out-fun way.

360 degree Photobooth

360 Photo Booth, can capture a slow motion video from all angles. Guests stand on a platform using a slow motion arm to circle automatically around them. You can also customize overlays and animations, there will be awesome contents that can be shared within seconds.

360 Degree freeze booth

Imagine 20 cameras around you in 360 Degree circle, and all the cameras click you simultaneously.

Magic Mirror photobooth

Magic mirror photobooth is retro, smart and super fun. A perfect IOT tool for a generation that loves to share and enjoys getting clicked.

Magazine Photo booth

Magazine Cover Photo Booth let your guest take away a gift, which is a famous magazine cover with their photo on it. They will cherish it forever.

Photo Mosaic Wall

The Photo Mosaic Wall creates digital & physical mosaics from live event photos in real-time.
